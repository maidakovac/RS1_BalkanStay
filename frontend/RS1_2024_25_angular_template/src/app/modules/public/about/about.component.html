ABOUT US

Welcome to BalkanStay,
Your gateway to an unforgettable experience in the heart of the Balkans!

OUR STORY

Founded by two passionate students from Bosnia and Herzegovina, BalkanStay was born out of a shared vision – to enhance and promote tourism across the Balkans.

Our platform connects travelers from around the world with unique accommodations, offering an authentic and seamless way to explore this breathtaking region.

Whether you're dreaming of a cozy mountain retreat, a seaside escape, or a charming city apartment, BalkanStay makes it easy to find and book the perfect stay.

OUR MISSION

We aim to support local communities, create sustainable tourism, and preserve the rich cultural heritage that the Balkans have to offer.


We are committed to providing:

Affordable and authentic stays that reflect the local culture.

Easy booking experiences with secure payment options.

Support for small businesses by promoting local hosts and their properties.


Our ultimate goal is to bring people closer to the heart of the Balkans, enriching their travels and supporting the region’s growth as a tourism hub.

WHY US

Easy Booking: User-friendly platform that lets you book your next Balkan adventure with ease.
